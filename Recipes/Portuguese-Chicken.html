Portuguese Chicken Recipe

This Type of Chicken is a really tasty and easy recipe, ideal for those who dont want to take so much time cooking or simply want to get a quickly and good meal

Ingredients

  1. 2 small whhole chickens
  2. 80ml fresh lemon juice
  3. 60ml olive oil
  4. 2 spoons Sweet Paprika
  5. 2 spoons Oregano leaves
  6. 2 spoons Brown Sugar
  7. 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  8. 1 red chilli, finely chopped
  9. 1 spoon salt
  10. Black Pepper
  11. 250g tomatoes
  12. 1 cucumber
  13. 1 red onion
  14. 55g kalamata olives
  15. 2 spoons olive Oil
  16. 1 spoons lemon juice
  17. 1 spoon dried Oregano

Steps

  1. Clean The Chickens And Chop It into Small Cubes
  2. Combine lemon juice, oil, paprika, oregano, sugar, garlic, chilli, pepper and salt in a bowl. Pour over the Chicken and mix it. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the fridge for 6 hours
  3. Preheat the oven to 200 C. Place chicken and marinade in a roasting pan, Roast in oven, basting occasionally with pan juices, for 40 minutes. When its done remove from oven and leave it rest 5 minutes.
  4. Make The Salad, cut all the vegetables and place it in a bowl, season with oil, lemon juice oregano, salt and pepper. Toss to combine
  5. Enjoy!
