Lasagna Recipe

  • Tomato

  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make The Meat Sauce
  2. Cook The Noodles
  3. Make The Ricotta Mixture
  4. Layer The Lasagna acoording to the recipe instruccions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
